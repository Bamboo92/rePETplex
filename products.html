Product Overview

Our hot-washed PET flakes come in clear and light blue, with moisture below 1 %. Washed with caustic soda and ready for immediate processing—no additional drying required.

Technical Specifications

Flake size 12 mm
Color Clear / Light Blue
Wash Hot friction washing with caustic soda
Moisture < 1 %
IV (Intrinsic Viscosity) 0.7 – 0.8 dl/g
Melting Temperature 255 ± 5 °C
PVC 0 PPM
Floatable < 30 PPM
Other impurities < 70 PPM
Bulk density ~ 400 kg/m³
Packing Big bag < 900 – 1000 kg
Big bag size 100 × 100 × 220 cm (H)
