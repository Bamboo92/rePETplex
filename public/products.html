Our Products

Washed PET Flakes

Our primary product is hot-washed PET flakes produced from recycled PET bottles. These flakes are ideal for industrial applications including polyester fiber production, packaging, and manufacturing.

Available Colors: Transparent (Clear) and Light Blue. Our sorting process ensures consistent color grading to match your specifications.

Quality & Processing: The flakes are cleaned, dried, and prepared with low moisture content—ready for immediate use in your processing equipment.

Packaging: Shipped in big bags holding 800–1000 kg each, with container loads typically around 18–20 tons.

Customization: We offer sorting options by color or material type and can adjust production parameters to meet your unique requirements. Contact us for detailed technical specifications.
