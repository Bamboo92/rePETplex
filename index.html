Leading Exporter of Washed PET Flakes

Recycled PET for immediate processing.

At rePET Plex, we supply premium-quality hot-washed PET flakes—shipped directly from our factory in Tunisia or our warehouse in Europe. Our PET flakes are available as a mix of clear and light blue, thoroughly cleaned and featuring low residual moisture—ready for immediate use in your production without any additional drying.

Our mission is to support your business with reliable supply and support our planet with sustainable practices. Every shipment is a step towards a greener future.
