Company Profile

RePET Plex is a European trading company based in Germany, founded in 2017. We focus on the import and distribution of hot-washed PET flakes for various applications in plastic processing.

Our core competence lies in directly supplying industrial customers across Europe with high-quality recycled materials.

Our Partnership

We work closely with an experienced manufacturer in Tunisia who has been producing recycled PET flakes since 2010. As a long-standing production partner, they supply us exclusively with high-grade PET recyclates.
