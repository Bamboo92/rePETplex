About Us

Company Background

Founded in 2010 by a team passionate about recycling, RePET Global has grown into a leading provider of recycled PET flakes. What began in Tunisia has expanded into an international operation, supplying partners across Europe and beyond.

We use advanced cleaning and processing technology to ensure high-quality flakes meeting rigorous standards for manufacturers. Our commitment to quality and sustainability has earned us a reputation for reliability.
